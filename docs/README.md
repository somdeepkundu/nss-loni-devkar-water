## Survey Overview

**Survey Name:** Land and Livestock Holding of Households and Situation Assessment of Agricultural Households

**Round:** 77th Round NSS  
**Period:** January 2019 - December 2019  
**Visits:** Visit 1 and Visit 2 (two visits to the same households)

**Coverage:** Rural areas of India (excluding Andaman & Nicobar Islands)

### Key Data Topics
- Land holdings (operational and owned)
- Livestock holdings
- Agricultural practices
- Crop patterns
- Water resources and irrigation
- Farm income and expenditure
- Non-farm income sources
- Household economic indicators

## Data Structure

The NSS 77th Round data is organized by:
- State and Union Territory codes
- District codes
- Taluka/Block identification
- Village identification
- Household level records

For Pune district analysis, use:
- **State Code:** 27 (Maharashtra)
- **District Code:** 27-03 (Pune)
- **Taluka:** Indapur (for Loni Devkar region, pin 413132)

## Key Variables for Water Productivity Analysis

From these documents, relevant variables include:
- Area under irrigation (hectares)
- Irrigated area by crop
- Source of water (well, borewell, canal, etc.)
- Irrigation method (flood, sprinkler, drip, etc.)
- Crops cultivated and area
- Productivity/yield by crop
- Water availability and usage patterns
